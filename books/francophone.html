Where to Buy Francophone Section Books

ABC Bookshop

Located in Downtown Bamenda. Offers both new and used textbooks for all Forms. Discounts available for bulk purchases.

Hours: Mon–Sat, 8am–6pm

Contact: 650-123-456

Success Book Depot

Specializes in Anglophone and GCE prep materials. Located near the main market.

Hours: Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm

Contact: 678-222-111

Lighthouse Books

Carries approved textbooks and novels for Literature. Delivery available in Limbe and Buea.

Hours: Tue–Sun, 10am–4pm

Contact: 690-789-123
