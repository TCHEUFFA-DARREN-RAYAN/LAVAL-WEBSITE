Staff & Faculty

School Leadership

Dr. James Anderson

Principal

Dr. Anderson has over 25 years of experience in education and holds a Ph.D. in Educational Leadership from Harvard University. He has been leading Laval Bilingual High School for the past 10 years, implementing innovative educational approaches and fostering a culture of excellence.

Dr. Marie Dubois

Vice Principal (Francophone)

Dr. Dubois is a distinguished educator with a doctorate in French Literature from Sorbonne University. She oversees the Francophone section of our school, ensuring high standards in French language education and cultural integration.

Prof. Robert Williams

Vice Principal (Anglophone)

Prof. Williams brings extensive experience in English education with a Master's degree from Oxford University. He leads the Anglophone section, focusing on maintaining high academic standards and implementing effective teaching methodologies.

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Dean of Studies

Dr. Johnson holds a Ph.D. in Curriculum Development and has been instrumental in designing our integrated bilingual curriculum. She oversees academic planning, assessment strategies, and educational quality assurance across both language sections.
