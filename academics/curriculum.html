Our Curriculum

Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education that develops both academic excellence and bilingual proficiency, preparing students for success in a global context.

Junior Forms Curriculum (Forms 1-3)

In the Junior Forms, our curriculum focuses on building strong foundations in both English and French, while developing essential knowledge and skills across a broad range of subjects. Students are immersed in a bilingual environment where subjects are taught in both languages throughout the week.

The Junior curriculum is designed to:

  • Develop strong literacy and numeracy skills in both English and French
  • Build a solid foundation of knowledge across core academic subjects
  • Foster curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities
  • Encourage creativity and self-expression through arts and activities
  • Promote physical development and healthy lifestyle habits

Senior Forms Curriculum (Forms 4-5)

In the Senior Forms, our curriculum becomes more specialized while maintaining the bilingual approach. Students prepare for internationally recognized qualifications while developing deeper knowledge in their chosen subjects. The curriculum is aligned with both British IGCSE and French Brevet standards.

The Senior curriculum aims to:

  • Deepen knowledge and understanding in core and optional subjects
  • Develop advanced analytical and critical thinking skills
  • Prepare students for external examinations and further education
  • Enhance bilingual proficiency in academic contexts
  • Foster independence and self-directed learning

Our Bilingual Approach

Our unique bilingual methodology integrates both English and French across the curriculum, creating a truly immersive environment.

Bilingual Methodology

At Laval Bilingual High School, bilingualism is not just about learning two languages; it's about learning through two languages. Our approach goes beyond traditional language instruction to create an immersive environment where students develop true bilingual proficiency.

Key features of our bilingual approach include:

  • Subject rotation between languages: Core subjects alternate between English and French throughout the week
  • Dual-language resources: Learning materials are provided in both languages
  • Language support: Additional support for students who need to strengthen either language
  • Cultural integration: Cultural aspects of both languages are incorporated into the curriculum
  • Balanced exposure: Approximately equal time is dedicated to each language

This approach ensures that students develop not just language skills but also the ability to think, analyze, and express complex ideas in both languages.
