Academic Overview

At Laval Bilingual High School, we provide a comprehensive education that combines academic excellence with bilingual proficiency, preparing students for success in a global world.

Junior Forms (1-3)

Our junior curriculum builds strong foundations in both English and French, with core subjects taught in both languages. Students develop essential skills while discovering their interests and talents.

Senior Forms (4-5)

In the senior forms, students follow a more specialized curriculum aligned with international standards, preparing for external examinations while maintaining bilingual proficiency.

Educational Philosophy

Our educational approach is guided by core principles that foster academic excellence, bilingual proficiency, and holistic development.

Core Values and Principles

At Laval Bilingual High School, we believe that education should be transformative, empowering students to become critical thinkers, effective communicators, and responsible global citizens. Our bilingual approach is not just about language acquisition but about developing a deeper understanding of diverse perspectives and cultures.

Our educational philosophy is built on the following principles:

  • Academic Excellence: Rigorous standards that challenge students to reach their full potential
  • Bilingual Immersion: Authentic integration of English and French across the curriculum
  • Global Perspective: Developing international mindedness and cultural understanding
  • Personalized Learning: Recognizing and nurturing each student's unique talents and needs
  • Character Development: Fostering integrity, respect, responsibility, and empathy

Through this approach, we prepare students not just for academic success but for meaningful lives as engaged citizens in an interconnected world.
