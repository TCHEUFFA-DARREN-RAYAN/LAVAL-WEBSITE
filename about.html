ABOUT

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Laval Bilingual High School has a rich history of providing exceptional bilingual education to students from diverse backgrounds. What began as a small institution with just 50 students has grown into a prestigious educational establishment serving over 12,000+ students today.

Our school was established with the vision of creating a truly bilingual learning environment where students could become fluent in both English and French while receiving a world-class education. Over the decades, we have continuously evolved our curriculum and teaching methodologies to meet the changing needs of our students and the global society.

Our Philosophy

At Laval Bilingual High School, we believe that education goes beyond academic excellence. Our educational philosophy is centered around developing the whole person – intellectually, socially, emotionally, and ethically.

We are committed to fostering critical thinking, creativity, and character development in our students. We believe that by providing a supportive and challenging environment, we can help each student discover their unique potential and develop the skills they need to succeed in an increasingly interconnected world.

Our Campus

Our 15-acre campus offers modern facilities, including high-tech labs, computer centers, spacious classrooms, and vibrant recreational areas—designed to support learning and growth.

The campus includes:

  • ✔ Modern classrooms with interactive technology
  • ✔ Science and computer laboratories
  • ✔ Library with extensive resources in both English and French
  • ✔ Sports facilities including a gymnasium and playing fields
  • ✔Chemistry and Physics well-equiped Labs
