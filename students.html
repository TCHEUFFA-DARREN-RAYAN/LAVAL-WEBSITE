Student Life

Life at Laval Bilingual High School extends beyond the classroom. Discover the vibrant community and diverse experiences that make our school special.

Student Council

Our student council plays a vital role in school governance, organizing events, and representing student interests. Elections are held annually, giving students the opportunity to develop leadership skills and make a positive impact on our school community.

Sports Teams

Our competitive sports teams participate in regional and national tournaments. From soccer and basketball to swimming and tennis, there are opportunities for athletes of all levels to participate, develop skills, and experience the rewards of teamwork and dedication.

Arts & Culture

Express yourself through our vibrant arts programs, including drama productions, music ensembles, visual arts exhibitions, and literary publications. Our annual Arts Festival showcases student creativity and talent across multiple disciplines.

Community Service

We encourage students to make a difference through community service initiatives. From local volunteering to international service trips, these experiences foster empathy, social responsibility, and global citizenship while making a positive impact on communities in need.
