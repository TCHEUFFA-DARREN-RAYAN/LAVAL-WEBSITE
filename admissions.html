Admissions

Admissions Process

Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our bilingual environment and contribute positively to our school community.

1

Application Submission

Complete and submit the online application form along with all required documents. Applications for the upcoming academic year open in October and close in February.

2

Entrance Examination

Qualified applicants will be invited to take our entrance examination, which assesses proficiency in both English and French, as well as mathematics and general knowledge.

3

Interview

Shortlisted candidates and their parents will be invited for an interview with our admissions committee. This helps us understand the student's motivation, interests, and suitability for our program.

4

Decision and Enrollment

Admission decisions are communicated within four weeks of the interview. Accepted students must confirm their enrollment by paying the registration fee and completing the enrollment forms.

Admission Requirements

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or passport copy
  • Academic records from the previous two years
  • Two recommendation letters (one from a language teacher and one from a mathematics teacher)
  • Recent passport-sized photograph
  • Application fee payment receipt

Selection Criteria

  • Academic achievement and potential
  • Language proficiency in English and French
  • Personal qualities and character
  • Extracurricular interests and talents
  • Alignment with school values and mission

Financial Aid

Laval Bilingual High School is committed to socioeconomic diversity and offers need-based financial aid to qualified families. Financial aid applications must be submitted along with the admissions application.
