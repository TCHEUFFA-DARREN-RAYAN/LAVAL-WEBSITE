Find Us

Our campus is conveniently located in the heart of the city, easily accessible by public transportation.

Directions

From the city center, take the Metro Line 1 to Education Station. The school is a 5-minute walk from the station. Follow the signs for Laval Bilingual High School.

Parking

Visitor parking is available on campus. Please use the main entrance and follow signs to the visitor parking area.
